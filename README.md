# 🌍 Global Layoffs Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📖 Project Overview

This project explores the **Global Layoffs Dataset** using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to uncover workforce reduction trends across countries, companies, industries, and years. The analysis focuses on extracting meaningful business insights through data cleaning, visualization, and interpretation.

---
## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze global layoff trends across different dimensions.
- Identify the most affected countries, companies, and industries.
- Explore year-wise layoff patterns and company stages.
- Generate business insights using data visualization.

---
## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

---
## 📊 Analysis Performed

- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Top 10 Countries by Total Layoffs
- Top 10 Companies by Total Layoffs
- Top 10 Industries by Total Layoffs
- Year-wise Layoff Trend
- Funding vs Total Layoffs
- Company Stage Analysis
- Companies with 100% Workforce Layoffs

---
## 🔍 Key Insights

- The United States recorded the highest number of layoffs among all countries.
- 🏢 Amazon experienced the largest workforce reduction in the dataset.
- 🏭 Retail and Hardware were among the most affected industries.
- 📈 Layoffs peaked in 2023 before gradually declining in the following years.
- 💼 Post-IPO companies accounted for the highest share of layoffs.
- 📉 No strong relationship was observed between funding raised and workforce reductions.
